SPRINT 1
Wallet Tier System
Foundation Active — Ready to Build

Adds the equity_tier column to Supabase, wires it into the cron snapshot write and KV cache, and surfaces tier badges and a filter on the leaderboard plus a tier breakdown section on the discovery page. SPRINT 5
Wallet Alerts + Paper Copy Trading
Full Product Waiting on Sprint 1
Two parts. Alerts: follow any wallet, detect open/close/resize events between polls, show in a notification drawer with unseen badge. Paper copy trading: when a followed wallet opens a position, log a virtual paper position at the detection price and track its PnL in real time. All localStorage-first — designed so Sprint 6 auth swaps the storage backend without touching components.

Wallet Tiers — Approved Definitions

Elite $5,000,000+
Major $1M – $4.99M
Large $500k – $999k
Mid $100k – $499k
Small $50k – $99k
Micro $1k – $49k
Dust Under $1k (noise floor, excluded from aggregations)
