Wallet Tiers — Approved Definitions

All badges are the same uniform style — no color differentiation between tiers. Brand uses red and green only.

Elite $5,000,000 and above
Major $1,000,000 — $4,999,999
Large $500,000 — $999,999
Mid $100,000 — $499,999
Small $50,000 — $99,999
Micro $1,000 — $49,999
Dust Under $1,000 — noise floor, excluded from aggregations
