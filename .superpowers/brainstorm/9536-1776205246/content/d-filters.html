Design D — Filter Variants

Five treatments for direction + coin filtering. Sidebar uses a dot indicator instead of count badge — enough to signal activity, nothing to repeat in the popover.

F1 Color-coded pills · Top coins + inline search LONG turns green, SHORT turns red when active. Coin filter shows the 6 highest-volume coins as quick-tap chips, then a search input for anything else.
