# HyperliquidFLOW — Product Overview

HyperliquidFLOW is a real-time trading intelligence dashboard for Hyperliquid perpetuals. It tracks a curated set of high-performing wallets — the Smart Money Chads — and surfaces the patterns in their behavior as actionable signals, regime context, and divergence alerts.

Everything runs on a 60-second refresh cycle. No AI inference. No paid data feeds. Rule-based throughout.

---

## What It Does

The dashboard answers one question continuously: what are the wallets that have proven themselves on Hyperliquid actually doing right now, and what does that mean for the market?

To answer it, the system:

1. Maintains a cohort of Smart Money Chads selected by performance criteria
2. Snapshots their positions and fills every 60 seconds
3. Runs nine signal recipes against each snapshot delta
4. Scores and surfaces the strongest signals with EV estimates
5. Detects divergences between smart money and retail positioning
6. Generates rule-based trade plans for divergence candidates

## Pages

### Overview

The landing page. Shows the current state of the smart money cohort at a glance:

- **Smart Money** — how many wallets are active in the cohort
- **Active Signals** — count of signals fired in the last cycle
- **Market Vibes** — current regime: Bull / Bear / Choppy / Breakout
- **Smart Money Exposure** — net directional bias (long vs. short notional)

Below the stats, the top signals from the last cycle are listed with coin, recipe name, direction, and EV score. Wallet addresses link directly to their profile in the Wallets page.

---

### Daily Brief

A narrative summary of the last 24 hours. Generated once per day on a cron schedule.

Covers:
- Smart Money health (cohort size, churn, performance)
- Top Smart Money Movers (biggest PnL gainers/losers in the cohort)
- Dominant signal recipes
- Regime transitions
- Notable divergences

This page does not update intraday. It is a fixed snapshot written at the daily cron boundary.

### Wallets

Three tabs in one page: Leaderboard, Discovery, and Lookup.

**Leaderboard** — ranks the current Smart Money Chads by composite score. Columns: rank, address, 30d PnL, win rate, Sharpe proxy, drawdown score, composite score. Clicking an address opens the full wallet profile in the Lookup tab.

**Discovery** — scanner for wallets outside the current cohort that are showing signals of breakout performance. Useful for identifying candidates before they are promoted to the cohort. Same click-to-profile behavior.

**Lookup** — full wallet profile. Enter any Hyperliquid address, or arrive via a link from Leaderboard/Discovery/external share. Loads immediately on arrival — no extra click required.

Profile contents:
- Verdict badge (Elite / Strong / Developing / Weak)
- Account summary: balance, leverage, total PnL
- 30-day stats: trades, win rate, avg PnL per trade, max drawdown
- Open positions table: coin, side, size, entry price, unrealized PnL
- Trade history: last 50 closing fills

### Signals

Live feed of signal events from the last scan cycle. Each signal was generated by one of nine recipes running against the latest snapshot delta.

Columns: coin, recipe, direction (LONG/SHORT), EV score, confidence bar, timestamp.

**EV Score** is normalized to [0, 1] using:

```
EV = (WinRate × AvgWin) − (LossRate × AvgLoss) − TradeCost
```

Scores above 0.6 are considered actionable. The confidence bar is a visual encoding of the same value.

Signals can be filtered by recipe, direction, and minimum EV threshold.

---

### Recipes

Reference page documenting all nine signal recipes. Each entry shows:

- Recipe name and description
- Historical win rate (from backtest)
- Average EV score
- Logic summary

Backtest data is derived from closing fills only — unrealized PnL is never counted.

**The nine recipes:**

| Recipe | What it detects |
|--------|----------------|
| **Size Up** | Smart money adding to a winner — position growing while PnL is positive |
| **Exit Watch** | Smart money reducing a large position — possible distribution |
| **Fresh Entry** | New position opened with no prior exposure on this coin |
| **Flip Signal** | Direction reversal — was long, now short (or vice versa) |
| **Crowded Trade** | Many cohort wallets entering the same coin in the same direction simultaneously |
| **Funding Fade** | Positioning against an extreme funding rate — fade the crowded side |
| **Breakout Rider** | Entry following a clean price breakout above recent range |
| **Liquidation Magnet** | Price approaching a cluster of known liquidation levels |
| **Divergence Alert** | Smart money and retail positioned in opposite directions with high confidence |

### Imbalance

Detects divergences between smart money and retail positioning in real time.

For each coin where a significant imbalance is detected, the page shows:

- Smart money direction vs. retail direction
- Confidence score (0-100%)
- Reason string explaining the divergence
- Optional: Smart Trade Plan

**Smart Trade Plan** is generated on demand when you click a divergence row. It uses ATR-derived levels to produce:

- Entry zone
- Stop loss
- Take profit
- Risk/reward ratio
- Kelly-suggested position size
- Current funding rate

This plan is rule-based and not financial advice. It is a starting point, not a recommendation.

## Scoring Model

Wallets are scored on four factors, each normalized to [0, 1]:

| Factor | What it measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Sharpe Proxy** | Return per unit of volatility over the last 30 days |
| **PnL Consistency** | How evenly distributed wins are across time (vs. one lucky trade) |
| **Drawdown Score** | Inverse of max drawdown — lower drawdown, higher score |
| **Market Vibes Fit** | How well the wallet's directional bias aligns with the current regime |

Composite score is a weighted average. The weights favor Sharpe Proxy and PnL Consistency.

Wallets scoring above the cohort threshold are eligible for Smart Money Chad status. The cohort is re-evaluated on a rolling basis — wallets that decay below threshold are replaced by candidates from the Discovery scanner.

## Data Mechanics

**Source:** Hyperliquid public API — no authentication required, no paid tier.

**Refresh cycle:** 60 seconds. Every cycle:
1. Fetch open positions and recent fills for all cohort wallets
2. Diff against the previous snapshot to generate a SnapshotPair
3. Run all nine recipes against the SnapshotPair
4. Score new signals, discard duplicates, cache results in Vercel KV
5. Update regime classification

**Regime (Market Vibes):** Classified from the aggregate net delta of the cohort. If >60% of notional is long-biased: Bull. >60% short-biased: Bear. High dispersion: Breakout. Otherwise: Choppy.

**Backtest methodology:** All win rates and EV estimates are computed from historical closing fills in the dataset. Unrealized PnL is never used. Each recipe's performance is tracked independently.

---

## Remarks

- Cohort composition changes over time as wallets are promoted and demoted. Historical signal performance reflects the cohort that was active at the time.
- Funding rate data is fetched independently and may lag by up to one cycle.
- The Smart Trade Plan uses ATR from the last 14 candles on the 1-hour chart. In low-volatility markets, the resulting levels may be very tight.
- Discovery scanner candidates are not in the cohort — their signals are not included in the main feed until promoted.
- All times shown are UTC.
